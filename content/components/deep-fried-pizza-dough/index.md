ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups flower
- 1/2 (7g package) of dry active yeast
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 TBSP sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup warm water
- 1 TBSP oil (I use coconut oil)

directions:
- Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
- Add wet ingredients and stir until mixture is thick and even
- Let dough sit for one hour so it can rise.
